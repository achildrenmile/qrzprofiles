About JOTA-JOTI

Every third weekend of October, half a million Scouts across 170+ countries connect via amateur radio (JOTA) and the internet (JOTI). It’s the biggest event in Scouting — bridging borders through radio waves and digital links.

OE8JOTA is the special event station from Carinthia, Austria — operated by the Jambuleers on the Air, bringing scouts and amateur radio operators together on the bands.
